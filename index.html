ABOUT US

It is no coincidence that the Canary Islands are called the Hawaii of Europe - this is an ideal place for surfing fans: stable warm winds all year round, sandy beaches, kilometers of untouched open spaces and clear seas. And it becomes a real Mecca for surfers. Fuerteventura is the second largest island of the archipelago.

This is the best place in Spain to learn to surf. In addition to the huge Atlantic waves that come here in winter and autumn, Fuerteventura also boasts many shallow, calm lagoons, perfect for learning beginner surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available all year round - there is always waves and suitable weather here. Moreover, both a real pro and a beginner can find waves for their level. That is whay the place is best suited for learning. here it is possible to move to the next level without difficulties.

While listing the advantages, one cannot ignore the fact that Fuerteventura’s beaches are the longest in the Canaries. As a result, it is impossible to imagine them overcrowded with tourists. You will only come across those who, like you, will have one goal - learning to surf.

You may just want to try surfing or you may be an experienced surfer. Our surf school can offer training for any skill level.

Four hours of training a day will help you conquer the waves with confidence and feel like a real surfer.

The main advantages of our school are the high quality of training and organization of classes, safety (all our clients are insured by Allianz), excellent equipment provided for the entire course of study, including soft safe boards for beginners.

First, we evaluate the level of surfing skills and physical fitness. After that we draw up a training program. We can assure you that after the training is over, you will not only get a lot of great sensations, but you will take your skills to the next level. a new level.

WE OFFER

Accommodation

Our surf school is located 100 m from the ocean coast. It features an outdoor pool and free Wi-Fi. The upper terrace offers ocean views. Distance to city center 15 min.

Flight &
insurance

All program participants are insured by Allianz insurance company. Flights from Tenerife to Lanzarote and back are included in the price of the program.

Training &
equipment

All equipment and supplies are from well-known manufacturers and are provided for the entire course of the program. The training process begins with determining your riding level.

A huge advantage of our school is its own training methodology that allows you to get on your board as quickly as possible.

The school is sertified by Canary Islands Surfing Federation - CANARIA DE SURF. This means that our school meets all international standards. Recertification is carried out annually.

SURFING WITH US

Our surfing school coaches are among the best surfers in the Canary Islands. One of them them is the European longboard champion. They will happily share their experience with both beginners and more experienced surfers. All members of our staff are certified members of the Canary Islands Surfing Federation.

Our coaches regularly participate in seminars and advanced training courses held throughout the world by various well-known surf clubs and federations. Therefore, in our surf school you can be sure you are trained by the best coaches!

The surfing school method is designed for all levels: beginners, intermediate-level surfers, and TOP-level surfers. We offer group trainings and personal coaching.
